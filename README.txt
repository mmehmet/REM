REM - Home automation bash scripts for your lounge room
=======================================================

Most files are documented with whatever you need to modify to get it working
for your own setup. Hardware/software used here are CM19 X10 modules:
http://pr0gr4mm3r.com/linux/getting-your-x10-cm19a-module-working-in-linux/

and the Festival text-to-speech system:
http://www.cstr.ed.ac.uk/projects/festival/
